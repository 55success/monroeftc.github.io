About Cyber Titans

Cyber Titans is a competitive FIRST Tech Challenge team centered in Monroe Township. Students learn by doing: designing robot systems, building mechanisms, writing code, testing ideas, fixing failures, and improving until performance shows up on the field.

01

Monroe-Based, Not Monroe-Only

The team is based in Monroe, but participation is not limited to Monroe students. We welcome students from neighboring towns and nearby communities who are serious about robotics and want to contribute.

What Students Do

This is not a passive club. Students build, test, analyze, improve, and compete.

M

Mechanical Design

Build robot mechanisms, work with motors, gears, structure, motion, intake systems, lifts, drivetrains, and precision assembly.

C

Coding & Control

Program autonomous and driver-controlled behavior, integrate sensors, tune performance, and learn how software directly affects match results.

S

Strategy & Competition

Study the game, choose priorities, evaluate tradeoffs, test ideas, and develop a robot that is built to score, survive, and win.

Join the Team

We are looking for students who are curious, disciplined, and ready to work on something challenging. Interest in robotics helps, but commitment matters more than prior experience.
