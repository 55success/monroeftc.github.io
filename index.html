About Cyber Titans

Cyber Titans is a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team based in Monroe Township, New Jersey. Students design, build, and program competition robots while learning real engineering skills.

While the team is Monroe-based, we welcome motivated students from nearby towns and surrounding communities who are interested in robotics, programming, engineering, or design.

What Students Do

Mechanical Design

Students build advanced robot mechanisms using motors, gears, and precision engineering.

Programming

Robots are programmed using Java and sensors to compete autonomously and driver-controlled.

Strategy

Teams analyze game rules and design robots optimized to win competitions.

Innovation

Students learn real-world engineering, teamwork, and problem solving.
